### 1. **Warm Red**
   - **Hex**: `#E63946`
   - **Inspiration**: The rich reds found in Ethiopian spices and stews (like Berbere and Doro Wat).

### 2. **Golden Yellow**
   - **Hex**: `#F1C40F`
   - **Inspiration**: The vibrant yellow found in turmeric, which is used in various Ethiopian dishes.

### 3. **Earthy Brown**
   - **Hex**: `#8B4513`
   - **Inspiration**: The earthy tones of lentils, teff (used in injera), and the natural landscapes.

### 4. **Fresh Green**
   - **Hex**: `#27AE60`
   - **Inspiration**: The greens from fresh vegetables and herbs used in Ethiopian cuisine.

### 5. **Deep Maroon**
   - **Hex**: `#800000`
   - **Inspiration**: The deep, rich color of some Ethiopian stews and the traditional clothing.

### 6. **Neutral Cream**
   - **Hex**: `#FDFEFE`
   - **Inspiration**: The lighter color of injera and a neutral to balance the vibrant colors.

### 7. **Bold Orange**
   - **Hex**: `#E67E22`
   - **Inspiration**: The bright, bold colors found in Ethiopian spices and textiles.

### 8. **Rich Purple**
   - **Hex**: `#4A235A`
   - **Inspiration**: The royal and rich aesthetic of Ethiopian culture and textiles.

### Example Palette:
- **Primary Colors**: Warm Red (`#E63946`), Golden Yellow (`#F1C40F`), and Earthy Brown (`#8B4513`).
- **Accent Colors**: Fresh Green (`#27AE60`) and Bold Orange (`#E67E22`).
- **Neutral/Base Colors**: Neutral Cream (`#FDFEFE`).
- **Dark Accent**: Deep Maroon (`#800000`) or Rich Purple (`#4A235A`).
